Our Story

The Producers You'd Never Find on the Tourist Trail

Canopy Crates is the product arm of Canopy Collective — bringing the authentic flavours, makers, and character of the Cairns and Daintree region directly to boutique hotel guests and AirBnb visitors.

Every crate is an invitation to experience the region beyond the tourist trail. Selected, packaged, and delivered personally by Michelle — someone who knows which valley the best honey comes from, and which producer nobody else has heard of yet.

Our Producers

Meet the Locals

We partner with passionate artisan producers from across the Daintree and Far North Queensland. Every product tells a story of this remarkable region.

Daintree Estates

Tree-to-Bar Chocolate

Australia's first and only tree-to-bar chocolate, grown and crafted in the Daintree since 2011.

Daintree Tea

Australian Black Tea

Pure Australian-grown tea from the Cubbagudta Plantation, pesticide-free since 1978.

Kefir Queen

Fermented Foods

Small-batch, handcrafted ferments from Port Douglas. Water kefir, kombucha, sauerkraut, and more.
🍷 Epicurean

Epicurean Port Douglas

French Provincial Fine Foods

Artisan terrines, cured meats, French cheeses, and curated wines at the heart of Port Douglas.
🍇 Shannonvale

Shannonvale Winery

Tropical Fruit Wines

Award-winning mango, passionfruit, and lychee wines crafted from organically grown fruit near Port Douglas.

Fenglehorn Sauces

Award-Winning Chilli Sauces

Handmade from 100% Queensland-grown chillies. Multi-award-winning, including a national first place.

Daintree Wellness

Natural Skincare

Natural wellbeing products inspired by Indigenous wisdom and Australian native botanicals from the Daintree.
